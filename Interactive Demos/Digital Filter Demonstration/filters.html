Frequency Domain

This is a demonstration of some basic audio filters, using white noise as an input. Turn on audio and alter the Filter Frequency slider to hear and see the effect of the currently-selected filter on that white noise input.

Select different filter types using the Filter Type selector, and listen to the effect they have on the audio.

Note how the filters here do not remove all frequency content beyond their cutoff frequencies; rather, they attenuate that content with a visible slope.
